What

We invite you to join us for a chance to dance, learn and make new friends. Please contact us if you have any questions concerning our group or West Coast Swing dancing.

Where & When

We meet on alternating Sunday afternoons at the Riolo Dance Studios.

2pm - 3pm Group Lessons

3pm - 6pm Open Social Dancing

$10 for members & non members

$5 for full time students w/ valid ID

$20 membership fee (entitles to discounts)
